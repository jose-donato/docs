Economy

overview​

Description:​

Provide a market overview of a variety of options. This can be a general overview, indices, bonds and currencies. [Source: Wall St. Journal]

Usage:​

usage: overview [-t {indices,usbonds,glbonds,currencies}]

Parameters​

NameDescriptionDefaultOptionalChoices
typeObtain either US indices, US Bonds, Global Bonds or CurrenciesNoneFalseindices, usbonds, glbonds, currencies
