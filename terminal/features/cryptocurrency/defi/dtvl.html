dtvl

Displays historical TVL of different dApps. [Source: https://docs.llama.fi/api]

Usage​

usage: dtvl -d DAPPS

Parameters​

NameDescriptionDefaultOptionalChoices
dappsdApps to search historical TVL. Should be split by , e.g.: anchor,sushiswap,pancakeswapNoneFalseNone
