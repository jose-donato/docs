ugs​

Description:​

Print up to 25 undervalued stocks with revenue and earnings growth in excess of 25%. [Source: Yahoo Finance]

Usage:​

usage: ugs [-l LIMIT]

Parameters​

NameDescriptionDefaultOptionalChoices
limitLimit of stocks to display.5FalseNone

Examples​
