est​

Description:​

Yearly estimates and quarter earnings/revenues. [Source: Business Insider]

Usage:​

usage: est [-e {annualrevenue,annualearnings,quarterearnings}]

Parameters​

NameDescriptionDefaultOptionalChoices
estimateEstimates to getannualearningsFalseannualrevenue, annualearnings, quarterearnings
