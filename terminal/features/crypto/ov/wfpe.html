Overview

wfpe​

Description:​

Coin withdrawal fees per exchange [Source: https://withdrawalfees.com/]

Usage:​

usage: wfpe [-c COIN]

Parameters​

NameDescriptionDefaultOptionalChoices
coinCoin to check withdrawal fees in long format (e.g., bitcoin, ethereum)bitcoinFalseNone

Examples​


2022 Feb 15, 08:21 (✨) /crypto/ov/ $ wfpe
